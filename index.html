How it works

  • Sign Up

    We ask that you sign-up to use the full capacity our technology. It’s way for you to introduce yourself.

  • Get a personal assistant from the get-go

    As soon as you sign up, we will pair you with a Personal assistant to help you with your search. She will help you in every step of the process of buying or renting.

  • Search real time listings

    Search the home you want using a set of criteria that will help narrow your search. We use real time interactive map to help you.

  • Get a dedicated Home61 Agent

    As soon as you select Homes you wish to see, we will pair you with a HOME61 Agent that will advise and help you.

    Select the home you wish to visit by clicking on the “contact to visit” button. In minutes, your Personal Assistant will call you to verify your selection and organize the visits according to your schedule.

  • Get recommandations

    Your Personal Assistant working with your HOME61 Agent will help you find your perfect home.

    Your HOME61 Agent will help you organize your closing seamlessly.
